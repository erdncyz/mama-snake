# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Mamba Snake ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Process

Mamba Snake does not require a traditional account. Firebase Authentication creates a random anonymous identifier so online features can work securely. We may process:

- The nickname you choose, solo and co-op scores, reached level, and score timestamps.
- Online room state, movement directions, and temporary gameplay snapshots needed for co-op play.
- App interaction events such as game starts, game mode, completed levels, and leaderboard views.
- App version, device and operating-system information, diagnostics, crash reports, and performance-related technical data.
- Advertising identifiers only when available and permitted by your device settings and consent choice.

We do not request your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, or payment information.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to provide online multiplayer and leaderboards, remember game preferences, prevent unauthorized database access, understand feature usage, maintain service reliability, diagnose crashes, and display or measure advertising.

Some preferences and high-score data remain on your device. Nicknames, leaderboard results, multiplayer room data, analytics events, and diagnostics may be transmitted to external services.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Google services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Remote Config, Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob. These providers may process technical identifiers and usage data under their own privacy terms:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

Remote Config controls app behavior but does not store your nickname or score. Crashlytics is used for diagnostics and is not used as a leaderboard or profile service.

## Data Retention and Choices

Multiplayer room data is operational data and may remain until it is removed during maintenance. Best scores may remain on leaderboards until deleted. Analytics and crash data follow Google Firebase retention settings.

You can deny App Tracking Transparency permission from iOS Settings. The game remains playable, though ads may be less personalized. To request deletion of an anonymous leaderboard record, provide the nickname and relevant score details through the contact link below; additional verification may be required to avoid deleting another player's record.

## Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly request names, email addresses, or other direct contact information from children. Parents or guardians who believe a child submitted personal information as a nickname can contact us to request review or deletion.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
